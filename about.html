About Us

Welcome to ACS College Of Engineering!!!!

"To gain quality, industry-relevant engineering education in a modern, well-equipped environment that nurtures innovation, research skills, and ethical leadership for a successful global career."

Vision:

To become a center of excellence in engineering education that nurtures innovation and sustainable progress.

Mission:

  1. Provide high-quality and relevant engineering education.
  2. Encourage research, creativity, and community service.
  3. Foster ethical leadership and lifelong learning skills.

Infrastructure Overview

  • Labs: Fully equipped computer, electronics, robotics, and physics labs.
  • Smart, air-conditioned spaces with projectors and interactive whiteboards.
  • Sports Facilities: Indoor arena, outdoor basketball and volleyball courts, and a dedicated gym.
  • Hostel: Secure, Wi-Fi-enabled accommodations for male and female students, including common rooms and dining facilities.
